About SDG 8

Sustainable Development Goal 8 aims to promote sustained economic growth, productive employment, and decent work for all people.

Problem in Juba South Sudan

In Juba, South Sudan many young people face unemployment due to:

  • Lack of access to digital and technical skills
  • Few job opportunities in the local area
  • Limited career guidance and mentorship
  • Poverty and lack of business startup support

Proposed Solution

This project introduces a digital platform to empower youth by:

  • Providing free online training (IT, entrepreneurship, digital skills)
  • Connecting youth with job opportunities and internships
  • Offering mentorship programs
  • Supporting small business ideas and innovation

Project Details

Project Name: Youth Empowerment for Economic Growth

Developer: Athian Atem

Location: in Juba, South Sudan

Objective: To reduce unemployment by equipping youth with skills and connecting them to opportunities.

Expected Outcome:

  • Increased employment among youth
  • Improved digital literacy
  • Growth of small businesses
